Media 1.
Multiplicity

Drawn in collaboration with Projective Architecture Office

The atlas of migrant worker housing at Foxconn’s Longhua Campus was drawn by interpolating CAD and GIS data sets, dozens of publications and theses, published and personal interviews, images and maps from Google, Apple, and Baidu search engines, on-the-ground photography, and site visits.

Shenzhen, China, 2019
Client: Seoul Biennale of Architecture and Urbanism
Architectural design: Projective Architecture Office
Creative Team: Yujun Yin, Stephen Zimmerer
