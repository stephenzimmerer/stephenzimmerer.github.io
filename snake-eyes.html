Shed 1.
Snake eyes

A shed built for a queer cocktail bar in Brooklyn. An addition to an existing row home, built to create privacy between the interior of the house and the bar’s backyard. An assembly of lumber, galvanized steel, polycarbonate, and hardware.

Brooklyn, New York, 2023
Client: Singers Bar
Architect: Stephen Zimmerer
Creative Team: Stephen Zimmerer, Mike Guisinger
